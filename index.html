What is Responsbl?

Responsbl is a little side project I created to help with my own mental battles in life. I wanted something to occupy my mind and test my abilities while also helping others with their own mental struggles. I learned through many hours of self-help books, self-reflection meditation that we are Responsible for our own lives. It sounds simple but, it's really not. Many of us fail to grasp that we are truly responsible for every aspect in our lives, even the things outside of our control. I may get hit by a bus and lose a limb and yes the person driving is still responsible as-well but, you are responsible for how you choose to react to that situation in your life. You could let it defeat you or you could let it empower you.
